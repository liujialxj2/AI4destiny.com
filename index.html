Hand Landmark Detection

The system uses MediaPipe technology to identify 21 key points on your palm, including:

  • Wrist base point (point 0)
  • Thumb's 4 joint points (points 1-4)
  • Index finger's 4 joint points (points 5-8)
  • Middle finger's 4 joint points (points 9-12)
  • Ring finger's 4 joint points (points 13-16)
  • Pinky finger's 4 joint points (points 17-20)

Through the relationships between these key points, the system can:

  • Calculate palm shape and width ratio
  • Measure finger lengths
  • Precisely locate the three main palm lines
